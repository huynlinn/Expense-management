ABOUT ME

Now I'm a sophomore and studying IT. Although I think this subject is quite fascinating, my personality is shy and introverted, which sometimes makes it difficult for me to work in groups and I'm also afraid to ask questions when I have problems. That is why I sometimes find it difficult to fully understand complex courses.

In addition, I often feel pressured to deal with difficult lessons and worry that my slower learning pace might put me at a disadvantage compared to my peers. In fact, when I first started my major, it was so difficult that I thought about dropping out.

Thanks to the help of friends around me, who have always been behind me to encourage and help me when I have difficulties, so I use that as motivation to study and strive to rise up every day. As I continue to learn and grow, I find myself loving the subject more and more. Actually, I have planned that when I finish the program and graduate, I will study abroad to improve my education.

In general, although I still face challenges from time to time, I believe that as long as I try, persevere and be patient, I will definitely succeed on the path I choose.
